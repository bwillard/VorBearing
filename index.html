VOR Bearing Finder

If you every find yourself with GPS coordinates and talking to aviation minded people (e.g. you are HABing and talking to the FAA), you will probibly need to conver your Lat/Long into a bearing from a VOR.

The goal of this site is to allow you to enter GPS coordinates and get a bearing and a distance from a VOR station.

The VOR station list come from http://ourairports.com/data/ I have no idea how acurate or up to date it is, so please don't sue me if it isn't righ tin your area.

You can enter the Lat/Long in DMS or decimal format, or you can right click on the map to set the coordinates.
